About Us

B.Y.O.B. (Build Your Own Band) was created for people who love music and those who love playing music. Life happens and sometimes people need to find another person for a music gig or venues need a band. We (Scott, Shane, Kelvin and Juan) felt the need for this and wanted to create a spot where people can find each other to make music.
